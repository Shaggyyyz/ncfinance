Feedback & Complaints

Your satisfaction is important to us. Here's how we handle your concerns.

We value your feedback and are committed to resolving any concerns you may have efficiently and fairly.

Internal Dispute Resolution (IDR)

If you have a complaint, please contact us first so we can try to resolve it:

  • Email: info@ncfinance.com.au
  • Phone: 1300 385 222

We will acknowledge your complaint generally within 1 business day and aim to provide a final response within 30 days (standard complaints) or 21 days (certain disputes involving default notices).

External Dispute Resolution (EDR)

If we cannot resolve your complaint to your satisfaction, or if the timeframes above have expired, you may refer your complaint to our external dispute resolution scheme, AFCA. This service is free to consumers.
