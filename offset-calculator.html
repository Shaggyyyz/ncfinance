How offsets fit into your strategy

Understanding offset accounts in practical terms

An offset account can be a powerful tool for some borrowers, but the benefits depend on your balance, spending habits and the type of loan you choose. This calculator offers a simplified view of potential savings only.

Daily balances and interest

Offsets typically work on the daily closing balance in the account. If your balance fluctuates a lot during the month, the actual savings may differ from a scenario where the same amount is kept in the account consistently, as assumed in this tool.

The results here assume the offset balance stays constant and that loan interest is calculated in a standard way for the entire term.

Suitability and product features

Some offset accounts come with higher rates or package fees compared to basic loans without an offset. Whether an offset is suitable depends on your ongoing balance and how you manage cash‑flow, not just the headline savings in a calculator.
